## Competition Info

* **Organizer:** Capybaras Team, Kuban State Technological University (КубГТУ)
* **Date:** May 1, 2026 10:00 ~ May 2, 2026 22:00 (MSK)
* **Format:** Jeopardy (task-based)
* **Categories:** Web, OSINT, Forensics, Stegano, Network, Crypto, MISC
* **Final Score:** 2779 pts / 7 solves

## Review

KubSTU CTF is an online CTF hosted by Kuban State Technological University in Russia.
The quality of the challenges was better than I expected, and they did a good job of reflecting real-world vulnerabilities.
However, the OSINT challenges were a bit tough since many of them required knowledge specific to Russia.
In the end, competing solo out of 810 participating teams, I finished **305th overall** and **177th in the Students division**.

## Conclusion

This was my first Russian CTF, and it turned out to be more enjoyable than expected.
The RepoForge challenge (a partnership problem) was especially interesting — chaining multiple vulnerabilities together felt very fitting for its INSANE difficulty rating.
I also came across HackAdvisor Labs, which seems like a great platform for practicing web hacking, and I plan to keep using it from now on.
